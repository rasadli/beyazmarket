About Us

Beyaz Market – This Is How the World Tastes

Welcome to Beyaz Market, your international grocery store in the heart of Bad Oeynhausen – where diversity meets quality and enjoyment feels like home. Under the motto “This is how the world tastes”, we’ve been bringing culinary treasures from around the globe directly into your kitchen for years.

Our family-run business stands for warmth, authenticity, and trust. As part of the vibrant community of the charming spa town of Bad Oeynhausen, it’s our heartfelt mission to provide our customers with fresh, high-quality products every day – always served with a smile.

Family-owned. Community-rooted. Quality-focused.

What You’ll Find With Us

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables in top quality – crisp, seasonal, and full of flavor.
  • A wide selection of Halal meat, carefully chosen and responsibly sourced.
  • International specialties, spices, teas, sweets, and pantry essentials from around the world – for authentic cuisine and special moments of indulgence.
  • Fair prices and regularly changing offers that make your shopping a pleasure.

We’re Here for You

We place the highest value on freshness, cleanliness, and customer service. At our store, not only are your wishes welcome – we also take the time to offer personal advice and thoughtful recommendations.

As a family business, we know how important trust, quality, and a friendly atmosphere are – and that’s exactly what awaits you every time you visit Beyaz Market.

“Whether you're searching for the traditional ingredients of your homeland or eager to discover bold new flavors – we have it ready for you.”

Beyaz Market is your number one destination for international food diversity in Bad Oeynhausen and the surrounding area.

Come by and get inspired – this is how the world tastes!
